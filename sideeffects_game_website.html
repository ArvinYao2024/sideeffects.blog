What is Side Effects?

Side Effects is a thought-provoking puzzle game that explores the concept of unintended consequences. Every action you take creates ripple effects throughout the game world, challenging you to think several steps ahead.

Navigate through intricate levels where your decisions matter not just for immediate progress, but for how they influence the entire game environment. Master the art of strategic thinking and discover that sometimes the indirect approach leads to the most elegant solutions.
